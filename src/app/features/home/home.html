From field to jar

  1. 01

    Bought

    Direct from farms around Guntur. No broker sits in between, which is why we can tell you whose field it came from.

  2. 02

    Dried

    Below 45°C, or in the sun. Heat is fast and cheap and it takes the colour and the smell with it. We go slow instead.

  3. 03

    Milled

    In batches small enough to watch. Big runs heat the powder as they grind, and you can taste the difference.

  4. 04

    Sealed

    The same day it is milled. Nothing is added at this step, because there is nothing we want to add.
